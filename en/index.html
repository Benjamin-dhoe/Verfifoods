Verfifoods makes the difference

Verfifoods is your specialist and wholesaler in bake-off and frozen products. After years of experience as a baker, Philippe Van Eeckhoudt deepened his knowledge in the bake-off industry. Verfifoods offers a wide range of frozen products.

Our range consists of over 250 different products, carefully selected with an eye for quality. To achieve this, our company collaborates with numerous Belgian and international suppliers.

At Verfifoods, we are convinced that we can make the difference.
